He is very handsome. He’s rather tall and slim. His eyes are blue. Clement has got short black, a bit curly hair. He has full lips and slim face. He is always well-dressed. NOTE FROM THE HERO

Clement  Maria  Prasan

Acting has so much to do with stories, emotions, drama, expressions, music and timing. An actor justifies his character on the screen or live and gets appreciated. Acting can only be done if the role has you as a part of it. If you do not involve and indulge into what the role demands then you will not be liked by the audience. There are many actors who don’t look that great but have managed to steal hearts of people through their acting skills. If you are a good actor then looks are a secondary concern to the audience.
